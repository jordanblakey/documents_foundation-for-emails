Page # Variable Item Explanation of Variability
1 Administrative Office Name/Address/Telephone Number/Internet Address Changed if administrative office location, phone number or website address changes.
1 Officers’ Signature and Title Changed if in the event an officer or the title of an officer signing the policy form changes, any new officer or title utilized will be an officer of the company.
Schedule Owner/Insured/Sex/
Policy Number/Policy Date		 Specific policy data will be based upon each individual’s information.
Schedule Issue Age Insured specific policy data will be based upon each individual’s information. The issue ages are 25-55 for the 10 year expiry period. The issue ages are 25-45 for the 20 year expiry period except for the Male Tobacco class which has issue ages of 25-43.
Schedule Premium Class Non tobacco classes are Super Preferred, Preferred, Standard. Tobacco class is Standard. There is a sub standard class (we are calling Flex) that will be lended for Tobacco and non Tobacco.
Schedule Mode of Premium Owner may choose to pay premiums annually or monthly.
Schedule Premium Guarantee Period The company offers two different premium guarantee periods of 10 and 20 years in length.
Schedule Expiry Date This is the date the policy will expire based on the premium guarantee period chosen.
Schedule Face Amount Variable based upon what the insured is approved for with minimum and maximum face amounts. The minimum amount is $5025,000and the maximum amount is $500,000.
Schedule Level Guaranteed Annual Premium This is the premium that is payable during the life of the insured to the end of the premium guarantee period. Premiums are guaranteed at policy issue.
Schedule Annual Policy Fee This is a one-time policy charge that will be included in the premium amount. Is there a minimum/maximum policy fee?
Schedule Administrative Office Changed if administrative office location or phone number changes.
Schedule State and Insurance Department and Phone Number The applicable state where this policy is issued and the department phone number will appear.
Schedule Electronic Funds Transfer Authorization This method of payment option will be shown if it is available.
Schedule Per Mode Premium and Total Yearly Premium The premium payments are variable based on each individual’s information. This will show the total amount of premium due yearly based on the mode chosen.
