{{#markdown}} {{> letterhead}} In Response to Your Life Insurance Application Dear First: Your life insurance application has been thoroughly reviewed. Regretfully, we are unable to issue coverage at this time due to your Motor Vehicle Report. Our decision was influenced by information obtained in a consumer report prepared by:
LexisNexis Consumer Center Attn: Life Reports P.O. Box 105108 Atlanta, Georgia 30348-5108 https://www.consumerdisclosure.com/
The consumer reporting agency did not make the decision to take action and cannot provide you with any reason(s) why the adverse action was taken. Under the Fair Credit Report Act, you are entitled to obtain a free copy of this consumer report directly from the consumer reporting agency. You can obtain a copy of the report by contracting them within 60 days of this notice. You are also afforded the right to dispute any of the information contained in the consumer report. You may do so by writing to the consumer report agency listed above. You have the right to know the item(s) of information that support the reason(s) given for this decision and the identity of the source of that information. If you ask us to correct, amend, or delete any information about you in our files and we refuse to do so, you have the right to give us a concise statement to what you believe is the correct information. We will put your statement in our file so that any authorized person wanting to review the file will have access to that information. If you would like additional information concerning this action, please send your written request to us at the New Business Processing Center Listed below. A notice at the end of this letter fully outlines your rights and explains other information about our procedures and practices. If you submitted money with your life insurance application in exchange for a Temporary Insurance Agreement, it will be promptly refunded. This letter and the refund void the Temporary Insurance Agreement. Please note, no life insurance coverage is or will be provided under the terms of the Temporary Life Insurance Agreement. {{> underwriter_signature}} Enclosure(s)
