{{#markdown}} {{> letterhead}} In Response to Your Life Insurance Application Dear First: Your life insurance application has been thoroughly reviewed. During the application process, we received information from MIB,Inc., a consumer reporting agency that operates an information exchange on behalf of insurers. Due to the information from this report, we are unable to proceed with the underwriting of your application. As the applicant, you will need to resolve or change any information from this report that is inaccurate. The consumer reporting agency (MIB,Inc) did not make the decision to take adverse action and cannot provide you with any reason(s) why the adverse action was taken. Under the Fair Credit Report Act, you are entitiled to obtain a free copy of this consumer report directly from the consumer reporting agency. You can obtain a copy of the report by contracting them within 60 days of this notice. You are also afforded the right to disbute any of the information contained in the consumer report. You may do so by writing to the consumer report agency.
MIB, Inc. 50 Braintree Hill Park, Suite 400 Braintree, MA 02184-8734 Telephone (866) 692-6901 — OR — Go to their website at https://www.mib.com and request your disclosure on-line.
You have the right to know the item(s) of information that support the reason(s) given for this decision and the identity of the source of that information. If you ask us to correct, amend, or delete any information about you in our files and we refuse to do so, you have the right to give us a concise statement to what you believe is the correct information. We will put your statement in our file so that any authorized person wanting to review the file will have access to that information. Enclosed is a notice that more fully outlines your rights and explains other information about our procedures and practices. Should you successfully correct your records with MIB, Inc., please feel free to reapply for coverage with our company. If you submitted money with your life insurance application in exchange for a Temporary Insurance Agreement, it will be promptly refunded. This letter and the refund void the Temporary Insurance Agreement. Please note, no life insurance coverage is or will be provided under the terms of the Temporary Life Insurance Agreement. {{> underwriter_signature}} Enclosure(s)
